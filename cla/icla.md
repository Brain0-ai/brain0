# brain0 — Individual Contributor License Agreement (ICLA)

> assignment to a future entity, Italian governing law, electronic acceptance via the
> CLA gate. Rationale and process: [`README.md`](./README.md), [`docs/open-core.md`](../docs/open-core.md).
