# brain0 — Corporate Contributor License Agreement (CCLA)

> Structured on the Apache CCLA model, adapted to brain0. Unlike the ICLA (electronic
> acceptance), the CCLA is executed in writing by an authorised signatory; the fields in
> the signature block and Schedule A are completed at signing. Rationale and process:
> [`README.md`](./README.md), [`docs/open-core.md`](../docs/open-core.md).

```
brain0 — Corporate Contributor License Agreement ("Agreement"), v1.0

Thank you for your interest in the brain0 project (the "Project").

This Agreement is entered into by the corporation or other legal entity
identified below ("Corporation") and the Project Owner (as defined in the brain0
Individual CLA, Section "Project Owner"). It allows the Corporation to authorise
Contributions submitted by its designated employees to the Project.

1. Definitions.
   "You" (or "Your") means the Corporation that is making this Agreement with the
   Project Owner.
   "Contribution" has the meaning set out in the brain0 Individual CLA.
   "Designated Employees" means the employees of the Corporation listed in
   Schedule A (as amended from time to time by the Corporation's CLA Manager).

2. Grant of Copyright Licence.
   Identical in scope to Section 2 of the brain0 Individual CLA, granted by the
   Corporation in respect of Contributions submitted by its Designated
   Employees, including the express right of the Project Owner to dual-license
   such Contributions under open-source, source-available, and
   proprietary/commercial terms.

3. Grant of Patent Licence.
   Identical in scope to Section 3 of the brain0 Individual CLA, granted by the
   Corporation in respect of Contributions submitted by its Designated
   Employees.

4. Representations.
   You represent that You are legally entitled to grant the above licences and
   that each Designated Employee is authorised to submit Contributions on Your
   behalf. You represent that each Contribution by a Designated Employee is an
   original creation (third-party submissions per Section 7 of the Individual
   CLA apply).

5. CLA Manager and Schedule A.
   The Corporation designates a "CLA Manager" who may add or remove Designated
   Employees by maintaining Schedule A. Amendments are made by written notice
   from the CLA Manager to the Project Owner (e.g. an update to the
   Corporation's Schedule A in the Project's signature registry). The Project
   Owner may rely on the most recent Schedule A received to determine whether a
   Contribution is covered by this Agreement.

6. Support, Warranties, Notification.
   Sections 5 and 6 of the brain0 Individual CLA apply mutatis mutandis to the
   Corporation.

7. Assignment.
   Section 8 of the brain0 Individual CLA (assignment to the entity formed to
   steward the Project) applies to this Agreement.

8. Governing Law.
   This Agreement is governed by the laws of Italy, without regard to its
   conflict-of-law provisions. The courts of Milan (Foro di Milano) shall have
   jurisdiction over any dispute arising out of or relating to this Agreement.
```
